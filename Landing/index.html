Regain control of your training data

Intro

FitEdit is a desktop app for viewing and editing training data.


Whether it be erroneous GPS data points, inaccurate heart rate, a missed lap key, a bad Strava KOM, or anything else, FitEdit is your go-to solution for viewing and editing training data.

Features

  • Beautiful and intuitive interface
  • Import and edit Garmin FIT, TCX, and GPX files
  • Visualize activity data with tables, charts, maps
  • Edit speed, heart rate, and other fields
  • Edit GPS points on a map
  • Merge, split, and trim
  • Export edited files
  • Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux
