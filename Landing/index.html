Have problems with your training data? FitEdit can help.

What is it?

FitEdit is a desktop app for viewing and editing training data.


Even if...

  • your device recorded bogus GPS data
  • your heart rate readout redlined for no reason
  • you missed a lap key
  • you pressed the lap key at the wrong time
  • you crammed multiple workouts into one activity
  • you spread one workout across multiple activities or devices
  • or had some other training data mishap

...whatever happened, FitEdit can help

Why use it?

  • Beautiful and intuitive interface
  • Import and edit Garmin FIT, TCX, and GPX files
  • Visualize activity data with tables, charts, and maps
  • Edit GPS points on a map
  • Edit speed, heart rate, and other fields
  • Merge, split, and trim activities
  • Export edited activities to file
  • Supports multiple sports, e.g. run, bike, swim, walk, and many others.
  • Supports Windows, macOS, and Linux
